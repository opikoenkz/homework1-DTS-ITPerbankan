Legendary Campursari Singer Didi Kempot

Biography

Personal life

Dhidhik Prasetya: born in Surakarta, Central Java, December 31, 1966 - died in Surakarta, Central Java, May 5, 2020 at the age of 53 years) or better known as his stage name Didi Kempot is a singer and songwriter campursari and congdut from Surakarta. He is the son of the famous traditional artist, Ranto Edi Gudel (known as Mbah Ranto), as well as the younger brother of Srimulat's senior comedian, Mamiek Prakoso.

Most of the songs he wrote have the theme of heartbreak and loss. The reason for deliberately choosing this theme is because the average person has experienced it and wants to be close to the community, is also the reason for Didi Kempot to use place names as the title or lyrics of his songs. Didi Kempot was an Indonesian singer and songwriter in the campursari style. He wrote some 700 songs, mainly in his native Javanese language. Apart from Indonesia, Didi was also popular in Suriname and the Netherlands, both of which have significant Javanese diaspora population.
